About Us

Empowering Smart Living and Security Solutions Since 2010

GVR Groups has established itself as a premier provider of smart home automation and electronic security solutions in Chennai and Coimbatore since 2010. As an authorized dealer for Control4, a leading USA-based brand in smart home automation, and other esteemed brands like Panasonic and GM Automation. Our team of experienced engineers is certified in Control4-USA, AMX-USA, Bticino-Italy, Merten-Germany, and Helvar-UK, ensuring expertise in a wide range of automation technologies.Specializing in lighting automation, curtain automation, video integration, and more, we cater to new constructions, offices, and retrofit projects. In the realm of electronic security, we provide comprehensive services including CCTV systems, digital door locks, fire alarms, motion sensors, and advanced automation solutions like floor cleaning robots and swimming pool cleaners. GVR Groups remains dedicated to transforming spaces with innovative technology and reliable service.
